Time Clock Help

Getting Started

On first launch, set your daily work targets in Settings → Time Clock. Then use the Time Clock tab to clock in/out and track your hours. Vacation and Sickness tabs are pre-configured with defaults but can be adjusted in Settings.

Time Clock

The Time Clock tab shows a grouped view of your work records by month and day. Use ▶ Clock In to start tracking and ■ Clock Out to stop. Add manual records via + Add Record. Double-click any record to edit. The header shows today's target, worked hours, and remaining balance.

Overnight Shifts

If end time is before start time (e.g. 22:00 → 06:00), the app treats it as an overnight shift. Duration is computed as (24:00 - start) + end - break. A warning is shown when saving.

Vacation

Track annual leave, public holidays, special leave, unpaid leave, and carry-over hours. The pool is calculated as allowance + carry-over credits minus used debits (annual, holiday, special). Carry-over from previous years is capped by your settings.

Sickness

Sick days are tracked in hours and converted to day-equivalents based on your daily target. Weekend sick days are capped at 1.0 day. Sick days reset each year.

Settings

Access via Settings → Settings or Ctrl+S. The dialog has five tabs:

Time Clock tab

SectionWhat it does
Daily Work Hours Enable each day with a checkbox and enter the target hours (e.g. 8.0 h). Disabled days count as rest days in the balance calculation. The day order follows the Week starts on setting in the Display tab.
Offices Manage the list of office locations that appear in the time-record form's "Office" drop-down. Add, edit, or remove locations here.
Break Presets Up to four break-duration presets (in minutes). These appear as quick-pick buttons when clocking out or editing a record, so you don't have to type the break length every time.
Default Work Type Pre-selects In Site, Road, or Remote when adding a new time record.
Overtime Sets the overtime rate multiplier (e.g. 1.5×) and the period over which overtime is calculated (week / month / year). These values are used in the overtime balance display.
Holiday Auto-Import Choose a country and year, then click Import Holidays for Year. Public holidays for that country are added directly to the Vacation tab as Public Holiday records with 0 hours — they appear in your calendar for reference but do not consume any vacation quota. Dates already recorded are skipped.

Date Exceptions tab

Override the standard daily work-hour target for a specific date. Use this for shortened working days (e.g. a half-day before a holiday) or for any day where the normal weekly target does not apply. Set Hours = 0 to mark the day as a non-working day entirely. The year filter at the top lets you browse exceptions year by year.

Vacation tab

FieldWhat it does
Year Select the year whose vacation settings you want to view or edit. Each year has its own allowance and carry-over cap.
Hours per year Your total annual vacation allowance in hours (e.g. 160 h = 20 days × 8 h). This forms the base of the vacation pool shown in the Vacation tab.
Max carry-over The maximum number of unused vacation hours that can be carried forward from the previous year. Surplus above this cap is forfeited when you run a carry-over transfer.

Sickness tab

FieldWhat it does
Year Select the year whose sickness settings you want to view or edit.
Days per year Your annual sick-day allowance (e.g. 10 days). Used to compute the remaining balance shown in the Sickness tab. Hours are converted to day-equivalents using the daily work-hour target.

Display tab

FieldWhat it does
Theme Switch between Light, Dark, or System (follows your OS preference).
Week starts on Sets whether weeks begin on Monday or Sunday. This affects the date-picker calendar layout and the order of days shown in the Daily Work Hours section of the Time Clock tab.

Export

Use File → Export to export records per tab. CSV is always available. Excel and PDF require optional dependencies.

FAQ

What happens if I close the app while clocked in?

On restart, the app detects open records and warns you. You can clock out or delete the dangling record.

How are overnight shifts handled?

If end time is before start, the app assumes the shift crosses midnight. Duration is computed across two calendar days.

Does the app handle DST changes?

All times are wall-clock local. The datetime('now') UTC function is used only for audit timestamps, not for clock-in/out times.
